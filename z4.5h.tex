$$\displaystyle{\lim_{x \to \infty}}\big(\frac{x^2+[2a]x+[c]}{x+[a]}\big)$$

$$\displaystyle{\lim_{x \to \infty}}\big(\frac{x^2([2a]+\frac{[c]}{x^2})}{x(1+\frac{[a]}{x})}\big)=\infty*[2a]=\infty$$
