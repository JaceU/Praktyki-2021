$$[m2]m^{2}+[b]=0 \iff [m2]m^{2}=-[b] \iff m^{2}=\frac{[b]}{[dm2]}=\frac{[l]}{[m]}=[w]$$
$$m_{1}=\sqrt{[w]}, \quad m_{2}=-\sqrt{[w]}$$
